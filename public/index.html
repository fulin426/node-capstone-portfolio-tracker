Portfolio Tracker

Keep track of assets all in one place

This tracker gives you a chance to view all your mutual funds in your portfolio at once. This will give you a chance to examine any changes and rebalance if necessary.

The primary goal of a rebalancing strategy is to minimize risk relative to a target asset allocation rather than to maximize returns. Portfolio rebalancing takes profits from higher performing assets and buying lower performing assets to bring the asset allocation to desired values.

A good rule of thumb is when asset class has changed by five percentage points. Periodic rebalancing along with a long term investing strategy can help reduce volatility and increase returns over the long term
